## RACI Format

This checklist uses a simplified RACI-style model for execution.

- `Responsible`: the person doing the work and driving completion
- `Approves`: the person who signs off before the item is considered done
- `Supports`: the person who provides inputs, assets, or technical help

## Status Legend

Use only these values in the weekly operating tables:

- `Not Started`: work has not begun yet
- `In Progress`: work is underway but not yet complete
- `Done`: work is complete and the completion gate has been met

## Six Green-Light Checks

All six must be true before Google Ads is paused.

- [ ] Booking-page-to-appointment conversion is materially improved and trending toward 22-28%
- [ ] Instagram and social traffic route directly to booking
- [ ] Google Business Profile has keyword-rich descriptions, 20+ photos, and weekly posts in place
- [ ] Yelp has at least 10 new reviews and a current profile
- [ ] Paid social retargeting audiences and tracking are live
- [ ] The calendar is 80%+ filled from organic sources for 2 consecutive weeks
