**Purpose:** Track features customers ask for that are missing. Use this to prioritize the roadmap.

## Current MVP Features (What's Working)

- Service & stylist selection
- Stripe payments (deposits)
- Admin dashboard
- Appointment management
- Client management
- Email confirmations (SendGrid)
- SMS confirmations (Twilio)
- Automated reminders (24hr + 2hr)
- Appointment reschedule/cancel
- AI chatbot (Claude)
- POS with Stripe Terminal

3. How do clients usually book? (Call, text, DM, website)

5. What's your biggest no-show/cancellation pain?
6. Do you track client hair photos/history?

## Competitor Analysis

| System | Pros | Cons | Price |
|--------|------|------|-------|
| Acuity | Easy setup | Generic, no loc focus | $15-45/mo |
| Square Appointments | Free tier, POS | Limited customization | Free-$65/mo |
| Vagaro | Full featured | Complex, expensive | $25-85/mo |
| StyleSeat | Marketplace built-in | High fees, no control | 25% commission |
| Booksy | Beauty-focused | Mixed reviews | $30/mo |

**Kelatic Differentiator:** Built BY locticians FOR locticians. AI assistant understands loc services. Integrated POS. Academy module for training.
